Information Technology

Ask the Expert: Gartner Top 10 Strategic Predictions for 2024 & Beyond

UPCOMING January 16, 2024
| 11:00 a.m. EST | 1 hour

Generative AI changed in 2023. Going forward, all areas of human endeavor will have to contend with genAI’s rapid evolutionary pace. Beyond AI, multiple technology innovations are overlapping to change the very nature of computing. Join this complimentary Gartner webinar as Gartner expert Daryl Plummer shares Gartner’s top 10 strategic predictions for 2024 and beyond, and answers your questions about the threats and opportunities these emerging trends could have on your organization. CIOs, tech providers and business leaders can ask how to determine which predictions will most impact them, and what they must do to act quickly on them.

  • Understand the personal and professional impact that generative AI has on people

  • Explore how businesses will get better at overcoming their worst traits

  • Discover how new threats create new responsibilities and communities


Return to this web page to watch the webinar. Contact us at gartnerwebinars@gartner.com with questions about viewing this webinar.

Hosted by

Daryl Plummer

Distinguished VP Analyst, Chief of Research, Gartner Fellow
